About

Adventures & Asanas is a personal blog that explores a particular lifestyle both on and off the yoga mat. Adventures & Asanas sends the message that yoga is so much more than just the physical practice and gravity-defying poses, it's truly a way of living in joy and exploring what the world has to offer.

Adventures & Asanas explores three main topics:

  • travel
  • nature

Sierra Duffey

Hello! My name is Sierra and I’m a yoga teacher in Ottawa.

A year into university I knew I needed to find an activity that would physically keep me in shape and help me deal with stress. With a yoga studio just down the street from my first apartment, it made sense to start there! I’ve been practicing regularly ever since, always coming back to yoga as a way to calm down, sustain my energy and bring out my best self.

A few years in, I knew I wanted to teach and allow others to explore yoga in their own way. My first 200-hr training program was the perfect way to bring my own voice to my sequences but also to teach in a way that gives space for students to make the practice for themselves.

With an insatiable lust for travel, a love of tea, good books and acoustic music, and a cheerful spirit, I’m excited to contribute back to the yoga community that gave me an entirely new love for life.

Private Lessons

If you're looking to deepen your personal practice, work with an injury or improve something specific, I offer private and semi-private lessons. Private lessons can be done in your home or mine and range from anywhere from 1 - 5 students.

Private lessons can focus on a specific style of yoga, or work towards more challenging postures or practices. Each student will complete an information form and we'll chat about your goals before the lesson!
